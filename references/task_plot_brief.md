# Task Plot Brief

## Task

- Title: Probabilistic Stimulus Selection
- Construct: reinforcement learning / probabilistic feedback learning / transfer bias
- Paradigm: learn AB, CD, and EF reward contingencies, then complete no-feedback transfer choices.

## Rows

- Train AB: A 0.80, B 0.20.
- Train CD: C 0.70, D 0.30.
- Train EF: E 0.60, F 0.40.
- Transfer: 15 pairings, no feedback.

## Trial Timeline

1. Block ready: 3000 ms before each block.
2. Choice screen: 4000 ms. Show two kana symbols. Press A for left, L for right.
3. Feedback: 1000 ms on learning trials only. Show correct or incorrect.
4. No-feedback transfer: transfer trials skip feedback.
5. ITI: 1000 ms fixation before next trial.

## Notes

- Learning repeats until criteria are met or 6 learning blocks are completed.
- Transfer presents 15 pairings with 10 trials per pair.
- Kana symbols are randomized into roles A-F per participant.
